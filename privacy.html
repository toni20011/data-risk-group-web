Privacy Notice

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

Data Risk Group LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at datariskgroup.com. This Privacy Notice explains what information we collect when you visit our site or contact us, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, email address, and company name when you submit a contact form
  • The content of messages you send us describing your situation or question
  • Information you share with us during calls or email correspondence

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, through standard web server logs and any analytics tools we use.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule calls
  • Provide advisory services you've requested
  • Communicate with you about your engagement with us
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (for EEA/UK Visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases: your consent (where applicable), our legitimate interest in responding to inquiries and operating our business, and the performance of a contract where we have or are entering into an advisory engagement with you.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for a reasonable period afterward in case you follow up. If we enter into a service engagement, we retain engagement-related records for as long as required by applicable law and professional obligations.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict our processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to data portability or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@datariskgroup.com.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

7. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed through a third-party form service (currently Formspree). Your submission is transmitted to and stored by that service in accordance with their privacy policy. We use Google Fonts for typography; Google may collect limited data per their own privacy policy when fonts are loaded.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated notice.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or our data practices, please contact us at:
