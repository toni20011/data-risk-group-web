Is AI working for your website
— or quietly against it?

Most small businesses are already using AI — to create content, drive traffic, build or update their website, and deliver services to clients. But few have stopped to check whether those tools are supporting the trust they've built, or quietly creating gaps: in how your site handles visitor data, how you represent your practices, and what your clients actually experience.

This quick assessment, developed by DRG partner Acorns AI Consulting, gives you a clearer picture of where you stand — starting with your website and the tools around it.
