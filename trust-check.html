The Trust Check.
A high-level look at your business.

A guided checklist across three areas: how you get leads, how you convert them, and how you deliver. Work through each section at your own pace. No technical knowledge needed. Takes about 5 minutes.

Want a deeper, live audit of your website specifically? After completing this checklist, you'll have the option to run a full website trust check via our partner tool, Data Pilots.

10 questions 5 minutes No signup required Instant results
Phase 1 Get Leads — Client Experience

01 / Someone finds you online for the first time. How quickly do they understand who you help and what you do?

02 / Do you have visible social proof on your website or social profiles: testimonials, reviews, case studies?

03 / Does your About page or "who I am" section help a potential client feel like they know you before reaching out?

Phase 2 Convert Clients — Transparency

04 / When someone fills in your contact or booking form, do they know what happens next: timeline, what you do with their information, what to expect?

05 / Does your website have a privacy notice or policy, and does it reflect how you actually work right now?

06 / If a potential client reads your offer or services page, can they tell how you handle their personal information during the engagement?

Phase 3 Keep Clients — Safety

07 / If a client asked you right now which tools have access to their information (email, project management, AI, file storage): could you answer with full confidence?

08 / Do you use AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or similar with client information? If yes, do you have a clear position on this you could explain to a client?

09 / When a contractor, VA, or team member leaves, or when you offboard a client: do you follow a process to revoke their access to your tools and files?

10 / If a larger client or corporate prospect sent you a questionnaire about your data practices, could you answer it confidently?
